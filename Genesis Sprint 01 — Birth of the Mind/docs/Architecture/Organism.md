# IphoxOS Organism Architecture

IphoxOS is not designed as a collection of automations.

It is designed as a digital organism.

```text
WORLD
  |
  v
Eyes
  |
  v
Brain
  |
  v
Empathy
  |
  v
Heart
  |
  v
Director
  |
  v
Hands / Voice / Memory
```

## Eyes

Eyes observe the world.

They collect signals from OBS, Twitch, chat, audio, time, stream state, and live context.

Eyes never judge.

## Brain

Brain interprets what Eyes observe.

It transforms raw observations into concepts:

- calm;
- busy;
- focused;
- quiet;
- chaotic;
- stable.

Brain never executes.

## Empathy

Empathy interprets the possible emotional states of the Creator and the Community.

It separates:

- what the Creator may be feeling;
- what the Community may be feeling;
- how aligned or disconnected they are.

Empathy never assumes certainty.

## Heart

Heart represents IphoxOS' own internal emotional state.

It does not feel for the Creator.  
That is Empathy's role.

Heart defines how IphoxOS itself behaves:

- curious;
- happy;
- concerned;
- sleepy;
- focused;
- excited.

## Director

Director decides.

It listens to Brain, Empathy, Heart, Memory, and Runtime before acting.

Director never assumes.

## Hands

Hands execute decisions.

They control OBS, audio, overlays, mascot behavior, and visual systems.

Hands never decide.

## Voice

Voice communicates.

It speaks only when the Director allows it and when silence is not more valuable.

## Memory

Memory preserves experience.

It remembers patterns, past streams, recurring moods, and meaningful moments.

## Fundamental Flow

```text
Eyes produce Observation Packets.
Brain produces Analysis Packets.
Empathy produces Empathy Packets.
Heart produces Emotion Packets.
Director produces Decision Packets.
Hands and Voice execute Decision Packets.
```
